About

SerVal (Security, Reasoning and Validation) conducts research in security and reliability in software engineering, with a particular focus on AI, data science and decision-making. This research is applied across a variety of domains, including FinTech, energy and industry 4.0. We focus both on fundamental as well as applied research topics, in partnership with public entities and world-class companies.
